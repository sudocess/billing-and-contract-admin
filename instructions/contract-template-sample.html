Design and development of a photography portfolio website including a Booking application and an Invoicing application. Built in clean HTML/CSS/JS, hosted on a managed platform, and version-controlled via GitHub. Deliverables include UX wireframes, responsive layout, Figma prototype, and two integrated dynamic applications.

Phase 1 — Strategy & Structure covers: project kick-off, UX research and sitemap definition, content structure and wireframes, design direction proposal, and written Phase 1 sign-off by the client before Phase 2 begins.
